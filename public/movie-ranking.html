The Best Movies According to Goodman

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Matrix (1999)

A hacker named Neo discovers that the reality he knows is a simulated world created by intelligent machines

Die Hard (1988)

A New York City detective, John McClane, finds himself trapped in a Los Angeles skyscraper, battling terrorists who have taken over the building during a Christmas party

Raiders of the Lost Ark (1981)

Indiana Jones, an intrepid archaeologist, races against the Nazis to find the Ark of the Covenant
